Less Prone to Staining

Will my teeth be straight after treatment?

The best way to clean your aligners is by brushing and rinsing them in lukewarm water and using special cleaning tablets. After eating it is important that you brush your teeth before you re-insert your aligners. This has two benefits; it keeps your teeth clean and stops food discolouring the aligners.

Invisalign® treatment is an orthodontic treatment method that provides many of our patients with an alternative to traditional braces. Invisalign® treatment uses a series of clear, removable aligners that have been custom made to your teeth. Generally you change the set of aligners you've been wearing once a week and over time your teeth move into their desired position.

We have used Invisalign® treatment in our practice since 2000. Our Blue Diamond Invisalign® status is in recognition of our experience using these aligners. The aligners are suitable for children, teens and adults, and in each case they’re comfortable to wear because they’re custom made to the shape of your teeth and jaws.
