How does Invisalign® work?

At the start of Invisalign® treatment, our Invisalign® expert uses state-of-the-art computer software to design a plan for moving your teeth from their current positions into the best possible alignment. With your treatment broken into several stages, we'll have you wear a different set of clear aligners every few weeks to accomplish gradual movement.

Your Invisalign® clear aligners should be worn day and night (minimum 22 hours per day) and you will remove them only to eat, drink, brush and floss. As you replace each aligner with the next in the series, your teeth will move, little by little, until they have straightened to the final position we have prescribed. You will come in for check-ups every 8-10 weeks to monitor your progress and to receive your next set of aligners.

Invisalign® treatment is an orthodontic treatment method that provides many of our patients with an alternative to traditional braces. Invisalign® treatment uses a series of clear, removable aligners that have been custom made to your teeth. Generally you change the set of aligners you've been wearing once a week and over time your teeth move into their desired position.

We have used Invisalign® treatment in our practice since 2000. Our Blue Diamond Invisalign® status is in recognition of our experience using these aligners. The aligners are suitable for children, teens and adults, and in each case they’re comfortable to wear because they’re custom made to the shape of your teeth and jaws.
